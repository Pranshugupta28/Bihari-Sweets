About Bihari Sweets

Bihari Sweets brand established by Shri Bihari Lal Gupta in 1950 with a clear vision of serving best in quality and purity. We have been satiating the taste buds of people residing in and around Chakghat Rewa MP

The business mantra of "Aahar Shuddhi Sattva Shuddhi" (Purity of Mind Follows Purity of Diet), is reflected at every aspect of our operations at all levels.
